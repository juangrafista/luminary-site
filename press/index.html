The pitch

Luminary is the only Mac app that turns the notch into a quiet source of daily inspiration. It runs from the menu bar, and on a schedule you choose, surfaces a handpicked quote over the MacBook's notch — a Dynamic Island for your desktop. Glance, read, get back to work.

Why it's interesting

Apple shipped the notch in 2021 and almost no third-party Mac app has done anything meaningful with it. Luminary is the wedge: a calm, beautifully animated overlay that lives where your screen already has dead pixels. No popups, no banners, no doomscroll loop — just one quote at a time, on your schedule.

Features

  • Notch overlay with auto-expand and auto-collapse animations
  • Six curated collections: Motivational, Coach Wisdom, Mindfulness, Literary, Stoic, and Mix It All
  • Thousands of quotes bundled offline — no network calls, no tracking
  • Save favorites and add your own personal quotes
  • Choose your cadence: Gentle, Regular, or Frequent
  • Menu bar app, no Dock clutter
  • $4.99 one-time Pro unlock — no subscriptions

About the developer

Luminary is built by Juan Valenzuela, a Lead Compositor and VFX Artist whose work spans blockbuster films and television including Sonic the Hedgehog 3, Alien: Romulus, Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny, and Guardians of the Galaxy. After years working in Nuke, Houdini, and machine-learning pipelines at top studios, he turned to building the small, polished Mac apps he wished existed — tools that feel quiet, considered, and at home on the platform. Luminary is his answer to a simple question: what would the notch look like if it cared about your day?
